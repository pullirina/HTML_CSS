What's New at Millenium Yoga

Workshops

Our workshops are designed to explain, demonstrate and practice particular set of poses, to address any health issue or to target specific areas of the body. The participants are given some tips, guidelines, and resources (if applicable) for later use. Attending workshops is an easy way for busy people to learn what is needed for them in a record time.

Are you interested in individual/small group (one-time or on-going) session at your convenient location and time? Contact Us
