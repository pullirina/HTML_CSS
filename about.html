About Millennium Yoga

We at Millennium Yoga believe in the holistic impact of Yoga on overall health thereby unfolding our true potential.

We coach people to be in touch with their inner core, and accompany them on their on-going journey to a self-fulfilling life. The classroom in struction, workshops, lectures, products, and coaching sessions are designed to match this philosophy.

People have a different understanding and expectations from Yoga. If you are the one who are looking at Yoga more as a lifestyle than just another form of exercise, then you will derive the maximum benefits from our classes and workshops. Millennium Yoga: Your partner on your way to a fulfilled and balanced life!
